# 📊 Sales & Customer Performance Dashboard | Tableau

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project presents a *dual-dashboard solution (Sales & Customer Analytics)* built using Tableau to analyze business performance and customer behavior.  

It focuses on transforming raw transactional data into *actionable insights* that support strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
To evaluate:
- Sales and profit performance  
- Customer growth and behavior  
- Product-level profitability  

and identify *key drivers of growth and areas of concern*.

---

## 🧠 Key Business Insight (Executive Summary)
> *Sales increased by 29%, but profitability is impacted by loss-making subcategories like Tables.*  
> *Customer base is growing steadily, with a small group of customers contributing significantly to total revenue.*

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- *Tableau* – Dashboard Development & Data Visualization  
- *SQL* – Data Analysis & Querying  
- *Microsoft Excel* – Data Cleaning & Preprocessing  

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Superstore Dataset (~10,000+ records)  
- Includes:
  - Sales, Profit, Quantity  
  - Customer details  
  - Product categories & subcategories  

---

# 📊 Sales Dashboard

## 🔍 Features
- KPI Cards (Sales, Profit, Quantity with YoY comparison)  
- Monthly trend analysis with highest & lowest markers  
- Sales vs Profit breakdown by subcategory  
- Time-series trend analysis (weekly performance)  

## 🔑 Insights
- Sales grew by *29.47% YoY, and profit increased by **32.74%*  
- Certain subcategories (e.g., *Tables) show **high sales but negative profit*  
- Profitability varies significantly across product categories  
- Sales peaks observed in later months, indicating seasonal trends  

---

# 👥 Customer Dashboard

## 🔍 Features
- Customer growth and order volume tracking  
- Sales per customer analysis  
- Customer order distribution (behavior analysis)  
- Top 10 customers contributing to profit  

## 🔑 Insights
- Total customers increased by *11.34% YoY*  
- Orders grew by *26.69%*, indicating higher engagement  
- Majority of customers place *4–8 orders*  
- Top customers contribute a significant share of total revenue  

---

## 💡 Business Impact
- Identifies *loss-making product categories* for corrective action  
- Enables *data-driven pricing and inventory decisions*  
- Supports *customer segmentation and retention strategies*  
- Improves overall *business performance monitoring through KPIs*
